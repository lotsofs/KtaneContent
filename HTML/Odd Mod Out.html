On the Subject of Odd Mod Out

Spot the difference.

Three images of a modded module will be displayed on the module when it activates. Two of these images are identical while the other is unique.

Press the button that corresponds to the unique image displayed. Pressing the wrong button will cause a strike and will not deactivate the module.
